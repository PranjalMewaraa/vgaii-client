## Internal notes for whoever's filling this in

- Keep the tone matter-of-fact. No marketing-speak ("revolutionary," "best-in-class," "AI-powered"). Doctors hate it.
- The ROI math should use **the prospect's actual numbers** from discovery. If you didn't get those numbers, go back and get them before sending.
- The "what's not included" section reads as confidence, not weakness — every section that lists what we *don't* do increases trust.
- Don't add testimonials until they're real. Empty quote boxes look worse than no testimonials.
- Always quote the **annual prepaid** price first. If they downgrade themselves to monthly, that's their choice — don't lead with it.
- Send as PDF (export from Google Doc / Notion). PDF over Word — locks the price.
- File name: `VGAII Proposal — [Clinic Name].pdf`. Don't include the date in the filename — they'll be sharing it internally and a date makes it look stale by the next meeting.
